About Archer Roose ▶▶

  • A perfect pairing

    Initially attracted to the brand through her love of wine, Elizabeth Banks stands by Archer Roose’s mission to make high-quality wine accessible to modern wine drinkers with formats to fit their lifestyle.

    Both Elizabeth Banks and Co-Founder Marian Leitner- Waldman are leaders in their industries, share similar values and are partnering together to elevate diversity and empower women in the beverage industry. They continue to challenge the status quo by creating a product that disrupts how wine is made and packaged, but also how it’s marketed - with humor.

  • About Elizabeth Banks

    Actress, producer, writer and director Elizabeth Banks is known for roles in both comedy and drama, as well as film and television. Her work includes stand-out performances in projects including The Hunger Games franchise, Love & Mercy, The LEGO Movie and Mrs. America. She is also a three-time Emmy nominee for her recurring roles in 30 Rock and Modern Family.

    As a filmmaker, Banks made her directorial debut in 2015 with Pitch Perfect 2, and produced the Pitch Perfect franchise through her production company Brownstone Productions, along with her husband Max Handelman.
